We are excited to celebrate more than one year since INSPIRE Lab launched in January 2025!
Over the past year and a half, our lab has grown from its early foundation into a vibrant research group. We are grateful to our alumni, Mike DellaFera, Ali Fayyaz, Nida Shah, Artur Alsina, Archana Sathiyamoorthy, Catherine Smith, Hernan Gonzalez, Nurahmed Multezem, Olaniyi Salami, and Matthew Wang, for their contributions and wish them the very best in their next chapters. We are also delighted to welcome new members, Gabriela Poulose, Vishal Senthilkumar, Nghia Truong,David Simberg, Zuzana Lucy, and Noah Hill, to INSPIRE Lab.
We are excited to share several recent updates:
INSPIRE Lab is proud to contribute to computational brain research as part of the University of Maryland Grand Challenges Impact Award, Predictive Biology Hub for Human and Environmental Health.
Congratulations to Archana, whose paper was selected for an oral presentation at the 2026 Northeast Bioengineering Conference!
Congratulations to Jianan, whose paper was selected for an oral presentation at the 2026 IEEE EMBC in Toronto!
We are also proud to celebrate INSPIRE Lab’s first appearance at OHBM 2026 in Bordeaux, France.
Thank you to everyone who has contributed to INSPIRE Lab’s growth. We look forward to continued discovery and new milestones ahead.
