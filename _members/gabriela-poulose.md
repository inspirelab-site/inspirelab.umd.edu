Gabriela Poulose is a bioengineering undergraduate student on the Pre -Med track. Her research interests are primarily in the application of engineering principles to understand human health and disease. She is particularly interested in translational research that bridges quantitative analysis through computational neuroscience, biomedical imaging, and clinical medicine to better patient outcomes. Outside of the lab she is involved with the University Honors Student Board, BMES, and is a mentor for Maryland Mentor Corps.
