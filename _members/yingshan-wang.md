I am interested in the information encoding and processing mechanisms within neural systems, with a particular focus on spatio-temporal activations. Driven by these biological insights, I aim to develop time-aware information processing and dynamic-control Artificial Intelligence inspired by the brain. My current research leverages mathematical tools and machine learning to model the brain as a dynamic system. This work aims to shed light on controllable neural disorders and uncover the mechanisms behind drug-modulated perception. In my daily life, I enjoy artworks and reading.
